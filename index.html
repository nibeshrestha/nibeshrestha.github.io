About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science at RIT. I am currently working in the area of Byzantine fault tolerant distributed consensus and Blockchains under the supervision of Prof. Kartik Nayak (Duke) and Prof. Pengcheng Shi (RIT). I obtained B.E. degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering from Institute of Engineering, Pulchowk, Nepal, in 2013.
